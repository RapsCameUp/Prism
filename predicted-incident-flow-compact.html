PRISM AI Reliability Platform
The Problem

Production incidents cost companies millions — teams are reactive, spending hours diagnosing issues that could have been prevented. PRISM solves this by predicting failures before they happen.

PRISM (Predictive Reliability Intelligence & Service Management) combines Splunk observability data via the MCP Server, Cisco CDTSM predictions, and Google Gemini AI to detect, investigate, and remediate production incidents autonomously — ingesting real-time telemetry, running predictive anomaly detection, orchestrating multi-agent investigations, and generating automated GitHub PRs with human-in-the-loop approval.
